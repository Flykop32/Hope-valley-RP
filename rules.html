Hope Valley RP rules

Replace these with your real rules (or paste them from Discord).

Core principles

  • Value of Life (VoL): act like your character wants to survive.
  • Value of Freedom (VoF): don’t force RP outcomes; create chances.
  • No powergaming / metagaming.
  • Stay in character in RP zones and during scenes.

Combat & crime

  • No random killing (RDM). Provide roleplay lead-up.
  • Robberies must have RP and an escape plan; no “rob & log”.
  • Respect lawmen scenes and negotiations.

Community

  • No hate speech or harassment.
  • Respect staff decisions; appeal calmly via tickets.

Tip: If your rules are long, keep a “Quick Rules” at the top for new players.
